Our final project is a program that generates unique soundscape compositions. It takes sounds from our library of field recordings and merges them to create a soundscape. The input text to the generator is the fingerprint for the composition it generates. Give it the same "seed", and it will produce the same composition.

This website provides a simple way to interact with our project. You can influence the generation of these randomized soundscapes by writing whatever you like. We recommend trying out your name, a paragraph from a book, or haphazard mashing on your keyboard.
